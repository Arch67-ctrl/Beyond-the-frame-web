DESCRIPTION

The CCS Photo Manipulation 2026 is a creative digital design contest where students showcase their artistic and technical skills in transforming images into visually compelling artworks based on a given theme. Participants will use photo editing tools to manipulate images, combine visual elements, and produce an original composition that effectively communicates the assigned concept.

The competition encourages creativity, visual storytelling, and technical proficiency in digital editing. Participants will be given a specific theme and time limit to conceptualize, design, and finalize their artwork before submitting their output to the judging panel. Expected outcomes include enhanced digital design skills, creativity in visual communication, and the ability to express meaningful ideas through digital imagery.

This project represents months of collaborative work, technical experimentation, and artistic exploration — all converging into one unified visual language.

General Objective

To enhance students' creativity and digital design skills through the creation of meaningful and visually engaging photo manipulations.

Specific Objective

To encourage artistic creativity and visual storytelling among students.
To develop participants' skills in digital photo editing and graphic design.
To promote the use of digital tools for creative expression and communication.
